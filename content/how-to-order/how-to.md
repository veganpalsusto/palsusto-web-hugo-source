## 1. Delivery dates
Orders are open as soon as concha days are announced on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/vegan.palsusto). If you do not have Instagram, [email us](../contact/) for delivery dates.

## 2. Go to our sales entrypoint
Go to our [online orders site](https://veganpalsusto.square.site/s/order?shipping=true#WOFXTM4FNJUY2B5DB4F3BX7F) to order. We have the stock, menus, and delivery options there on the site! 

Your order will be freshly produced, packaged & sent to arrive with Royal Mail's Tracked24 service!
